# Communication and Influence

These questions assess how effectively you communicate across different audiences and influence decisions, especially when you don't have direct authority.

## Questions

1. How do you communicate technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders?
2. How do you influence decisions when you don't have direct authority?
3. How do you present engineering roadmaps and progress to executive leadership?
4. Describe a time when you had to sell a technical idea to skeptical stakeholders.
5. How do you ensure alignment and clear communication across cross-functional teams?
6. How do you tailor your communication style depending on the audience?
7. Describe a time when miscommunication caused a problem. How did you resolve it and prevent it from recurring?
