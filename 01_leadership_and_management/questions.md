# Leadership and Management

These questions assess your ability to lead and manage a team effectively.

## Questions

1. How do you prioritize tasks and projects for your team?
2. How do you handle conflicts within your team?
3. Describe a time when you had to manage underperforming team members. How did you handle it?
4. How do you ensure your team stays motivated and productive?
5. How do you manage stakeholders' expectations while protecting your team from scope creep?
6. What's your approach to setting goals for your team? How do you track progress?
7. How do you balance individual growth and team performance?
8. How do you delegate effectively while maintaining appropriate oversight?
9. Describe a time when you inherited a struggling team. What did you do to turn things around?
10. What's your approach to giving difficult or critical feedback to a direct report?
11. How do you lead your team through ambiguity or uncertainty?
