# How do you handle scope changes or requirement shifts mid-sprint or mid-project?

## Why This Is Asked

Interviewers want to see that you can adapt to change without chaos. They're looking for a process to evaluate new requests, communicate impact, and re-prioritizeâ€”while protecting the team from constant context-switching.

## Key Points to Cover

- A process for evaluating and triaging change requests
- Communicating impact (timeline, scope, trade-offs) to stakeholders
- Deciding what gets deprioritized when something new comes in
- Balancing flexibility with predictability

## STAR Method Answer Template

### Situation
_[Describe the context - what was happening, what team/company, what was at stake]_

### Task
_[What was your specific responsibility or challenge?]_

### Action
_[What specific steps did you take? Be detailed about YOUR actions]_

### Result
_[What was the outcome? Use metrics where possible. What did you learn?]_

## Tips

- Give an example of a significant scope change and how you handled it
- Show you can say no or negotiate when change would be destructive
