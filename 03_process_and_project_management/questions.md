# Process and Project Management

These questions focus on how you manage software development processes and ensure timely delivery.

## Questions

1. What development methodologies have you used? Which one do you prefer and why?
2. How do you handle missed deadlines or delays in the project?
3. How do you ensure cross-team collaboration in larger projects?
4. Describe your process for conducting code reviews and ensuring best practices.
5. How do you manage dependencies between teams or projects?
6. What tools have you used for project tracking, and how do you ensure transparency in project status?
7. How do you handle scope changes or requirement shifts mid-sprint or mid-project?
8. What's your approach to risk identification and mitigation in projects?
9. How do you run post-mortems and incident reviews? How do you ensure follow-through on action items?
10. How do you balance speed of delivery with engineering quality?
