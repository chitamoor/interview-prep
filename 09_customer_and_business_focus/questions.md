# Customer and Business Focus

These questions assess how well you understand the intersection of engineering and business. Interviewers want to see that you can bridge the gap between technical execution and business outcomes, and that you make engineering decisions with the customer and business impact in mind.

## Questions

1. How do you ensure that your engineering efforts align with business goals?
2. How do you balance technical excellence with delivering business value quickly?
3. Describe a time when you made a technical compromise to meet a business deadline.
4. How do you translate business requirements into a technical roadmap?
5. How do you measure the customer impact of your team's engineering work?
6. How do you prioritize features vs. infrastructure investments?
7. Describe a time when you advocated for a technical investment that had long-term business value.
