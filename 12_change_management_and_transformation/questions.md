# Change Management and Transformation

These questions assess your ability to lead through organizational or technical change, drive transformation, and manage resistance.

## Questions

1. How have you led a major technology migration or platform transformation?
2. How do you manage resistance to change within your team or organization?
3. Describe a time when you had to restructure your team or significantly change processes.
4. How do you handle organizational changes (reorgs, leadership changes) that affect your team?
5. How do you build buy-in for a major change initiative?
6. What's your approach to phased rollouts vs. big-bang changes?
7. How do you measure whether a transformation initiative is succeeding?
