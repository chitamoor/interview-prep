# Scenario-Based Questions

These are hypothetical questions to assess how you would approach specific challenges.

## Questions

1. You find out that a critical system your team is responsible for will fail in 24 hours. What do you do?
2. Your team is consistently missing deadlines. What steps do you take to fix this?
3. You need to improve the performance of a legacy system while delivering new features. How do you approach this?
4. A key team member gives their two-week notice during a critical project phase. What do you do?
5. You're asked to cut your team's budget by 20%. How do you approach this?
6. Two of your senior engineers want to take completely different technical approaches to a critical project. How do you handle this?
7. You've just been promoted to manage a team that includes a person who also wanted the role. How do you handle this?
8. Your team's main product has a major outage during a holiday weekend. Walk me through your response.
