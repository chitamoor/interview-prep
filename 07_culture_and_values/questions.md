# Culture and Values

These questions assess alignment with the company's culture and values.

## Questions

1. What's your management style, and how does it align with our company culture?
2. How do you ensure that your team embodies the company's values?
3. What's your approach to fostering innovation within your team?
4. How do you ensure psychological safety in your team?
5. How do you build trust when joining a new team or organization?
6. How do you handle a situation where team culture diverges from company culture?
7. How do you encourage diversity of thought and constructive disagreement?
8. What role does work-life balance play in how you manage your team?
