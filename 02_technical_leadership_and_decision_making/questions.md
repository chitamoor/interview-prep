# Technical Leadership and Decision-Making

These questions assess your ability to make sound technical decisions and lead engineering efforts.

## Questions

1. How do you stay updated with new technologies, and how do you decide which technologies to adopt?
2. How do you handle technical debt while delivering new features?
3. Can you describe a time when you had to make a difficult technical decision? How did you approach it?
4. What's your experience with system design? Can you walk us through a system you designed or improved?
5. How do you ensure code quality and maintainability across your team?
6. What's your experience with scaling systems, and how have you approached it in the past?
7. How do you drive architectural decisions in a large organization with multiple teams?
8. How do you evaluate build vs. buy decisions for your team or organization?
9. How do you handle situations where the team is divided on a technical approach?
10. How do you balance innovation with reliability and stability in your systems?
