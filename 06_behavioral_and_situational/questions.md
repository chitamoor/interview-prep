# Behavioral and Situational Questions

These questions help interviewers understand how you handle real-world challenges.

## Questions

1. Describe a time when you faced a significant challenge as a manager. How did you handle it?
2. Tell me about a time you disagreed with a senior leader's decision. How did you handle it?
3. How do you handle feedback from your team?
4. Give an example of a time when you had to manage competing priorities.
5. Describe a project that failed. What did you learn from it?
6. Tell me about a time when you had to deliver bad news to stakeholders or your team.
7. Describe a situation where you had to pivot your team's strategy quickly. What happened?

9. Describe a time when you took a calculated risk. What was the outcome?
10. Tell me about a time you made a mistake as a leader. How did you recover?
