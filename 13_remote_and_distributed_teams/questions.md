# Remote and Distributed Team Management

These questions assess your ability to effectively manage teams that are geographically distributed, remote, or hybrid.

## Questions

1. How do you manage remote or hybrid teams effectively?
2. How do you maintain team cohesion and culture in a distributed environment?
3. What tools and practices do you use for asynchronous collaboration?
4. How do you handle time zone differences in a global team?
5. How do you ensure visibility into work progress without micromanaging remote employees?
6. How do you run effective meetings with distributed teams?
7. How do you build relationships and trust with team members you rarely see in person?
