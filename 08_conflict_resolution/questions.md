# Conflict Resolution

These questions assess how you manage interpersonal and professional conflicts.

## Questions

1. Describe a time when two team members had a conflict. How did you resolve it?
2. How do you handle disagreements with product managers or other stakeholders about priorities?
3. What's your approach when an engineer disagrees with a technical decision?
4. Describe a time when you realized you made the wrong call and had to reverse course. How did you handle it?
5. How do you mediate between engineering and product/business teams when they have conflicting goals?
6. How do you decide when to escalate a conflict vs. resolving it yourself?
7. How do you handle a situation where a high performer is also a source of team friction?
