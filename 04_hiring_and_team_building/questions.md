# Hiring and Team Building

These questions assess your ability to build and grow a high-performing team.

## Questions

1. What qualities do you look for when hiring engineers?
2. How do you ensure diversity and inclusion in your hiring process?
3. How do you onboard new team members?
4. Describe a time when you had to grow your team quickly. How did you manage it?
5. How do you retain top talent and keep engineers engaged?
6. How do you design an effective interview loop for engineering candidates?
7. How do you evaluate cultural fit (or cultural add) during the hiring process?
8. What's your approach to succession planning and building bench strength?
9. How do you build a cohesive team culture when bringing together people from different backgrounds?
